#### WeChat Work (WeCom) Configuration

1. In WeChat Work (Group), Right Click (Mac) or Click "..." (Windows) -> **Add Group Robot**.

![wechat_1](../images/image-20210903150833262.png)

2. **Create New Robot**. Name it "HFish".

![wechat_2](../images/image-20210903150857177.png)

3. Copy the **Webhook Key/Token**. (The part after `key=` or the full URL depending on HFish version requirement, usually just the key).

![wechat_3](../images/image-20210903150945171.png)

4. Paste into HFish, Test, and Save.

![wechat_4](../images/image-20210903152406368.png)

[Official Documentation](https://work.weixin.qq.com/help?doc_id=13376)
